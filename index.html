About Rohri.net

Rohri.net is the premier digital archive and information hub for Rohri, Sukkur, and surrounding areas in Sindh, Pakistan.

It preserves local history, heritage sites (especially the iconic Lansdowne Bridge 1889), culture, tourism information, and latest updates.

Maintained by Sayed Sajjad Hussain Musavi.
